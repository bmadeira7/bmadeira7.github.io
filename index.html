About Me

I am a passionate audio engineer who loves to code and work with technology. After graduating from Full Sail University, I began composing various audio works which led to my music being sold online through different record labels. I am in process of completing the full stack web development bootcamp at UNC Charlotte. I enjoy collaborating with other developers and solving problems. My goal is to combine my knowledge of audio production with my coding skillset to implement new and exciting ideas and solutions within the entertainment industry.
